About Instru-Rent

Instru-Rent is an instrument renting company from Water Valley, MS. The customer can rent out an instrument of their choice for up to 3 weeks! There are 10 different instruments on the site in all: pianos, acoustic guitars, electric guitars, conga sets, banjos, drum sets, violins, trumpets, saxophones, and clarinets.
